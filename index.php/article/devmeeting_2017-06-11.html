DevMeeting 2017-06-11

Submitted by aerbax on Sun, 06/11/2017 - 16:19

Highlights[ ]

  • Mooo has modified the way IP addresses are handled - making future I2P integration easier.
  • The getmonero.org website has added links to the Core GUI.
  • The new Kovri website is Live: https://getkovri.org/
  • Pigeons is working on a Github ticketbot to assist with day-to-day duties
  • MoneroMooo has completed his last FFS milestone! Congrats, Mooo! And thank you for everything that you've done....and we're excited to see what's coming next! Time for another FFS round.
  • MoneroMooo has started working with one of the Luigi's on MultiSig.
  • Next meeting will be on June 25th.
  • Gineropolous is continuing work on FluffyNet/FluffyBlocks (compact blocks)
  • tewinget is pushing to get the ZeroMQ(0MQ) work completed by the end of this month (June 2017).
