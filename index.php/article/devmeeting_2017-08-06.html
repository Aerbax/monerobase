DevMeeting 2017-08-06

Submitted by aerbax on Sun, 08/06/2017 - 16:16

Highlights[ ]

  • New default ringsize.
    • 5 has consensus, but 8 and 10 are being discussed.
    • Probably best if we can get input from our mathmeticians, Surae and/or Sarang
  • The *testnet* hard fork is scheduled for Monday, August 7th 2017
  • GUI News
    • The official GUI's Android and iOS are ready for alpha/beta testing. The holdup is app store accounts(at least within iOS).
    • Official Android builds are also held up by disk space on the build machine. 1.5 month old test build at: https://github.com/monero-project/monero-core/pull/780
    • Investigating iOS Testflight
    • For the MyMonero app, Endogenic "just wants to launch the damn thing"  ;)
  • Work is ongoing to fix a memory leak in monerod
