DevMeeting 2017-10-01

Submitted by aerbax on Sun, 10/01/2017 - 16:12

Highlights[ ]

  • The Monerujo Android wallet has been released
    • https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.m2049r.xmrwallet&hl=en
    • Source code: https://github.com/m2049r/xmrwallet
    • The community has heard no issues thus far, but it is important to treat this as an early release and only put in what you can afford to lose.
  • vtnerd posted a utility for performing wallet scan "time trials" using various techniques and crypto libs(mostly for MRL stuff):
    • https://gist.github.com/vtnerd/0337f7daf71b33d945a984171c478bca
    • Part of the utility implements "spend-public" decompression - which could improve wallet performance(12% suggested) with no crypto library changes.
    • Even though there are no library changes, the current codebase may need to be readjusted.
  • MRL plans to release a paper today regarding subaddresses - once formatting has been cleaned up.
  • Sarang continues research into aggregate signatures.
  • The Forum Funding System(FFS) is being redesigned to be integrated directly into the getmonero.org site.
    • Mockup: https://getmonero.org/forum-funding-system/
    • May need a rename. Community Funding System ?
  • Licensing needs to be discussed: https://github.com/monero-project/meta/issues/85
  • Purism (https://puri.sm) is currently interested in integrating Monero directly into their Debian (PureOS) builds as a default.
    • This would also mean that there could be a push into Debian's upstream codebase.
    • If anyone currently runs PureOS ( https://pureos.net/ ), the Monero dev team would like feedback as to how well Monero currently runs there.
  • There is a "Monero Month" planned in December
    • Monero will be at the CCC (34C3) - partnering with Bitcoin-assembly and riat-assembly. Discussions in #monero-ccc
    • Monero Vienna Meetup
