DevMeeting 2018-05-20

Submitted by aerbax on Sun, 05/20/2018 - 14:38

Highlights

  • Monero Integrations Team has released a payment gate way for OpenCart
    • https://github.com/monero-integrations/monerocart
  • Stoffu has added automatic popping of bad version blocks
  • rbrunner is working on an easier MultiSig implementation
  • sarang is analyzing an implementation of non-interactive refunds
    • https://www.sharelatex.com/read/vcyxgpntfsgz
  • BulletProof audits are still underway
  • Serhack is working on a Mastering Monero book
    • Preview: https://masteringmonero.com/book/preview.pdf
  • MyMonero desktop app is ready for testing.
    • https://github.com/mymonero/mymonero-app-js/releases/tag/v1.0.0-rc3
    • https://www.reddit.com/r/Monero/comments/8kumc0/mymonero_desktop_app_release_candidate_available/
  • Next meeting June 3rd, 2018
