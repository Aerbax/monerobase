DevMeeting 2017-07-09

Submitted by aerbax on Sun, 07/09/2017 - 16:17

Highlights[ ]

  • Mac Smart Mining PR#1936 closed in favor of OSX Background Mining
  • ZeroMQ nearly ready for commit . There may be some initial inconsistencies between the JSON RPC commands and ZeroMQ.
  • MRL is working on publishing a document to accompany SubAddresses
  • hyc would like some feedback on a license change for LMDB
  • Pigeons is going to review adding a docker file for Android NDK
  • Re-imagining the FFS(Forum Funding System)
    • We need to formalize it more
      • For example, overfunding. It currently goes to the general fund and is used for future FFS proposals
    • Milestones aren't always the best option for some projects.
    • Should people who didn't contribute get a say in how a problematic FFS is handled?
      • Mooo suggested he might be able to code some sort of voting platform to confirm who has donated.
    • How about people who contributed, but aren't really "qualified" to comment on technical issues?
  • gkruja has some screenshots for their Android Wallet: http://imgur.com/a/mluUb
